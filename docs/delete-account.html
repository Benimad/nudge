Delete Your Nudge Account & Data

App: Nudge — ADHD Habit Tracker & Focus (com.app.nudge)
Developer contact: adamlaalami72@gmail.com

You can delete your Nudge account and all associated data at any time, either directly in the app or by email. You do not need to justify the request.

Option 1 — Delete everything in the app (immediate)

  1. Open Nudge.
  2. Go to Settings (the gear tab).
  3. Under Privacy & data, tap "Delete all data".
  4. Confirm. Your data is deleted immediately.

Uninstalling the app afterwards removes anything remaining on the device.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Email adamlaalami72@gmail.com with the subject "Nudge account deletion request" from the Google account you used to sign in (or describe your account if you used the app anonymously). We complete deletion requests within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Deleted immediately by in-app deletion: all habits, completion history, streaks, mood check-ins, focus-session records, AI-coach chat history, notes, and your goals/profile — both the local database on your device and your private cloud copy (Firebase Firestore), including any live-presence record.
  • Deleted on request (email, within 30 days): the bare authentication record itself (your user ID, and the Google email/display name if you signed in with Google). In-app deletion removes all content but keeps this sign-in identity so the app continues to work.

What is kept, and for how long

  • Subscription records are held by Google Play / Apple and RevenueCat under their own policies (we never see payment details). Manage or cancel subscriptions in your app store account.
  • Anonymous analytics events (if enabled in your build) contain no habit content and are not linked to your name or email; they are retained under PostHog's standard retention and cannot be traced back to you after account deletion.
  • No other data is retained. There is no additional retention period for habit content — in-app deletion is immediate and permanent.

Questions

Anything unclear, or want confirmation your data is gone? Email adamlaalami72@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
